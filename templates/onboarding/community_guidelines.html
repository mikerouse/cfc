Community Guidelines

Building a respectful community

Council Finance Counters is a platform for constructive discussion about local government finances. These guidelines help ensure our community remains welcoming, informative, and productive for everyone.

✅ What we encourage

  • Evidence-based discussion: Back up claims with data and sources
  • Constructive criticism: Focus on policies and decisions, not individuals
  • Respectful debate: Disagree without being disagreeable
  • Helpful contributions: Share relevant knowledge and insights
  • Factual accuracy: Correct mistakes politely when you spot them

❌ What we don't allow

  • Personal attacks: No harassment, insults, or targeting individuals
  • Misinformation: Don't spread false or misleading information
  • Spam or self-promotion: Keep content relevant to council finances
  • Political campaigning: This isn't a platform for partisan politics
  • Discrimination: No content that discriminates against any group

⚠️ Reporting and moderation

If you see content that violates these guidelines:

  • Use the "Flag" button to report it to our moderation team
  • Don't engage with or respond to problematic content
  • Our volunteer moderators will review reports promptly

For users under 18: Comments and community features are restricted for your safety in compliance with the Online Safety Act. You can still access all financial data and comparison tools.
